Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Welcome to Cosmicore. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application at cosmicore.vercel.app.

1. Who We Are

Cosmicore is an independent productivity application created and operated by Aisha Sheikh. If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us at hellocosmicore@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the Cosmicore service:

  • Account information — your email address and display name when you register
  • Focus session data — duration, category, and task name of your focus sessions
  • Task and event data — tasks and calendar events you create within the app
  • Progress data — your XP, level, streak, and celestial objects you have forged
  • Mood check-ins — your daily mood selections, stored locally on your device only
  • Google Calendar data — only if you choose to connect your Google account. We access your calendar events solely to display them within the app. We do not store, modify, or share your Google Calendar data.
  • Payment information — if you subscribe to Cosmicore Pro, payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your card details. We receive only a confirmation of your subscription status.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to provide and improve the Cosmicore service:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To save your focus sessions, tasks, events, and progress
  • To calculate your XP, level, streak, and celestial collection
  • To manage your Pro subscription status
  • To send transactional emails such as password resets and email confirmation

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties except as described in this policy.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a hosted database platform. Data is stored in the Asia Pacific (Sydney) region. Supabase is compliant with industry-standard security practices.

Your mood check-in data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

5. Google Calendar Integration

If you choose to connect your Google Calendar, Cosmicore requests read-only access to your calendar events. This data is used solely to display your events within the app. We do not store, modify, delete, or share your Google Calendar data. You can disconnect Google Calendar at any time from the Settings page within the app.

6. Payments and Pro Subscription

Cosmicore Pro subscriptions (£4.99/month or £24.99/year) are processed by Stripe. When you subscribe, your payment information is handled directly by Stripe. We receive only a notification confirming your subscription is active. We never see or store your card number, CVV, or other payment details. You can cancel your subscription at any time from the Settings page.

7. Cookies and Local Storage

Cosmicore uses browser local storage to remember:

  • Your daily mood check-in
  • Your daily login streak tracking
  • Your session preferences

We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies of any kind.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — view all your data within the app at any time
  • Delete your account — permanently delete your account and all associated data from the Settings page. This is immediate and irreversible.
  • Export your data — contact us at hellocosmicore@gmail.com to request a copy
  • Correct your data — update your display name from the Settings page at any time

9. Children's Privacy

Cosmicore is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of Cosmicore after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
